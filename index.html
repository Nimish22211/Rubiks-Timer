Welcome to Your Rubik's Timer, By Nimish Bandha

Info:


It is primarily made for the solvers who can solve in under 2 minutes.
  • M: means minutes
  • S: means seconds
  • Ms: means milliseconds


HOW TO USE: To start the timer, use the 'enter' key and to stop it use 'spacebar', and to reset the timer use 0.

You can change the scramble by yourself by clicking on it, otherwise, it will change after every solve.

Average is calculated using all the solves in the solve section.

NOTE: once the average time will exceed the 2-minute mark, the average will change into seconds, so calculate it accordingly!
